My name is Steve Jones, I originally completed a City & Guilds 4 year apprenticeship in Carpentry & Joinery back between 1975 and 1979. I have worked within the construction industry ever since, working for various construction firms until I became involved in Aluminium Rainwater systems in 1997. My passion in this field led me to being an employee in this field for nearly 4 years from 1997. Here, I gained knowledge of the products and decided to form my own business, 'Metal Gutter Company' in 2001, which utilised my long-standing trade background. This was a new venture for me into specialist Aluminium Rainwater systems.

We provide products and quality workmanship regardless of the length of your request. We make use of superior metal gutter systems to suit domestic and commercial needs for Rainwater dispersal. Our company aims to guide you through your renevations of either new builds or period properties, supporting you in selecting the right rainwater system. There are many options and lots of scope for a sympathetic older style property or a sleek modern approach to new or unusual design projects.
